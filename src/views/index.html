Sons of Jamal Al-Musallamani for Trade

The group "Sons of Jamal Al-Musallamani for Trade" is a distinguished conglomerate operating in two main sectors: import and distribution as the first axis, and manufacturing and packaging as the second. The company was initially founded by Mr. Jamal Musallamani, with expertise dating back to 1952, an experience inherited through generations and still evolving and yielding results to this day.
